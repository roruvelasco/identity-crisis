Identity Crisis
CMSC 137 · Networked Game · AY 2025–2026
A multiplayer arena survival game where the safe zone shrinks, controls betray you, and only the last player standing wins.
◆  The Game
Identity Crisis is a networked top-down 2D arena game supporting a minimum of four players. Each round, a safe zone appears somewhere on the map. Reach it in time — or be eliminated. Starting at Round 3, the safe zone only fits n–1 players, guaranteeing one elimination per round. Chaos Events keep every round unpredictable: controls reverse, identities swap, and decoy safe zones flood the arena to create confusion. The last player standing claims the arena.
